Who are we

The journey of OHANA Private Resort began with the family’s vision of having a private pool, completed in 2013 but not yet open for rentals. Before the owner’s father passed away in 2012, he had invited relatives to visit and see the property. After his passing, the family continued to enjoy swimming there, but significant expenses led to financial losses during their visits. One owner proposed opening the resort as a business to offset these costs. While brainstorming names, they recalled a family reunion from around 2010 with the theme "ohana," meaning family, which emphasizes that family means nobody gets left behind or forgotten, including friends and loved ones. This concept inspired the name OHANA Private Resort. The resort officially opened for business in February 2014 and has since thrived, welcoming guests and creating memorable experiences.
